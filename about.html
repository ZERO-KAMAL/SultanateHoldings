About Sultanate Holdings

Aligning the shareholders with management to build lower middle market companies with deep industry experience, scale and realise their potential.
Sultanate Holdings LLC is a Chicago based Private Equity investment firm focused on leveraged buyouts and recapitalizations of smaller, lower middle market companies. Sultanate Holdings manages $8 Billion Investments.

Our Approach

We specialise in small and mid size business in developing countries and the opportunities they present and the challanges they face. We bridge the gap between the investors and business owners towards management excellence and achieving the desired ROI.

Investment Criteria

Company Size
  • Revenue of $5-100 million
  • Add-ons of any size
Business Characteristics
  • Small market niche leaders
  • Defensible market position
  • Growth potential –⁠ organically or through acquisition
Management
  • Management continuity is preferred, but not required
  • Family-owned or retiring founder

industry focus

  • Mining
  • Technology
  • Defence
  • Real Estate
  • Agriculture
